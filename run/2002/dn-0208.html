Uniforms
   Maggie Butterworth has uniforms!  Thank you Maggie!  $50 for a set of shorts and singlet.  If she does not have your size then place your order now. Those who do not have a uniform should wear any T-shirt that says DIANETICS, or wear whatever you have and a smile. 		Samurai 5K
   Ricki McGee, John Taussig, Andre Untiedt, Dick Orciuch, Mark Hamilton, Carla Moxon, Maggie Butterworth, Bert Joseph, Diane Christian, Maren Foley, Alex Nunn and Tim Mantis participated in this perrenial favorite.  Carla brought Tim, a team old-timer. With everyone in uniform we made quite an impact on the Little Tokyo scene. Finishers circled back and helped team mates finish. At one point we had six Dianetics runners in the finish chute at the same time. Well done guys!  A runner from New Orleans recognized DIANETICS from when he worked at Ground Zero and wanted his picture taken with us.
   We really felt like a team again, and people started picking up hats.  Carla will arrange for booksellers  for the next event and Andre will coordinate photography.
