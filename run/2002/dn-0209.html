Mt. Baldy Run To The Top 
   On short notice Carla Moxon, Taha Khodr, Andre Untiedt and John Radich showed up at Mt Baldy. Topping out at over 10,000 feet, this was an adventure! Really beautiful too. Andre made the mistake of trying to sprint the last 100 yards--and ran out of oxygen! They offered ski-lifts down, but you had to run down 4 miles from the summit to get to the ski lodge, so it was quite a workout.
   Carla presented DMSMH books to the organizers and Andre and Taha helped the organizers carry trash down from the finish.
   This race was so tough we're going to have to do it again!
