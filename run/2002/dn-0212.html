Say No To Drugs Holiday Classic 
   The incredible turnout of our team made this race memorable in its own right!  Even the race founder, Sandra Johnson, ran—and took 1st place!  Thank you everyone for amazing support for our race.  Perfect weather, superb organization and a swath of celebrities made for a thoroughly fun event.  Drug Free Marshals, TWTH and the Los Angeles Multathlon teams all swelled the ranks. We had many, many winners with Ricki McGee and Maggie Butterworth also taking 1st places; 2nd places for Shandor Torok, Crystal Apodaca, Pam Taylor, Sasha Laplaine; 3rd places Bartev Topalian (welcome back Bartev!), Jamie Butterworth, Rachael Ryerson, Diane Christian, Lisa Miller. The Jamie/Chris rivalry had Chris ahead this race.  See the edited version of the race results which lists (hopefully) everyone who participated.  And a big, big thank-you to Tara Lee Gerson and her team who organized the whole event.  We'll be back again next year!
Race results: http://www.saynotodrugs.com/la/
